Role: Sales Engineer

The Product Storyteller

The Goal:

Prove you can translate complex technical features into a "technical win" during the demo.

Core Content:

  • "The Art of the Demo" Video: A 2-minute "Micro-Demo" showcasing your ability to narrate a complex RPA or AI workflow with clarity and wit.
  • Customization Case Study: Describe a time you built a custom prototype or "Golden Demo" that specifically addressed a unique enterprise pain point.
  • A checklist of the technical discovery questions you ask to uncover "hidden" technical blockers.
  • Security & Trust Section: Highlight your experience answering security questionnaires and overcoming "IT Department" objections.
