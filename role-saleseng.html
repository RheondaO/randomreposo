Strategic Discovery (What we call 'Requirement Elicitation' & 'Scoping' where I come from)

These questions were synthesized using AI to ensure they align with Challenger and Command of the Message sales methodologies. I use AI to simulate stakeholder pushback, allowing me to refine the discovery, and present an acute and outcome-oriented approach.

  1. If your Account Executives could reclaim 5–10 hours a week currently spent on manual 10-K research, what specific high-value activities would you re-allocate that capacity toward to hit Q4 targets?
  2. In your current manual outreach workflow, what is the single biggest 'data gap' that causes a rep to lose confidence in a lead before they ever pick up the phone?
  3. When we look at our 'Lost-Closed' deals from last quarter, how many were due to a lack of personalization or failing to identify a specific executive 'Risk Factor' early enough in the cycle?
  4. How much friction is created when Marketing passes a lead that hasn't been enriched with current firmographic data? How does that impact the AE-SDR relationship?
  5. If we could automate the 'Pattern Interrupt' in every cold email using AI, what is the target increase in reply rates we need to see to justify this as a permanent part of the stack?
  6. What is the threshold for 'Creepy vs. Helpful' in your industry? How deep should the AI-personalization go before it becomes a risk to the brand voice?
  7. How do you currently measure the 'Time-to-Value' for a new lead? Does the manual research phase represent the largest bottleneck in that timeline?
