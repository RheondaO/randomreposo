  • Team Formation: Primary and supporting agents are dynamically assembled based on task requirements
  • Execution: Agents work in parallel or sequence, sharing context and intermediate results through the coordination layer
  • Review: Cross-agent validation ensures quality and consistency before delivery
  • Learning: All interactions and outcomes are captured in the knowledge base for continuous improvement

  • Detection: Pulse identifies anomalies and alerts Sentinel for triage
  • Analysis: Oracle analyzes patterns while Sentinel coordinates response team
