Taskade Battlecard

Company Profile

  • Founded: 2017
  • Headquarters: San Francisco, CA
  • Funding: ~$5M (Seed Stage, backed by Y Combinator S19, Grishin Robotics, TRAC, and Altair Capital)
  • Employees: 5-15
  • CEO: John Xie (Co-founder & CEO)

Product Stats

  • Users: Over 1 million+ global users
  • Free Tier: Yes (Generous features)
  • Pricing Tiers: Free, Starter ($6/mo), Pro ($16/mo), Business ($40/mo), Max/Enterprise ($200-$400/mo)
  • AI Features: Taskade Genesis framework (Vibe Coding — prompt-to-app generation), multi-agent orchestration, autonomous multi-agent loops, 15+ supported frontier models, custom workspace AI knowledge bases, and automatic AI model routing.
  • Export Options: Markdown, PDF, Plain Text, Image, HTML, and instant web app deployment/sharing link.
  • Integrations: 100+ native services via a durable workflow engine (Slack, Discord, Gmail, Google Calendar, HubSpot, Stripe, plus Model Context Protocol (MCP) support).
  • Mobile Apps: iOS, Android, Web

Key Features

  • Taskade Genesis (One-Prompt Apps): Users type what they want (e.g., "Build a client portal with an intake form and an AI assistant"), and Taskade generates the complete UI, backend database, automated workflows, and active AI agents.
  • Multi-Model Frontier Brain: Allows per-agent frontier model selection. Teams can assign Anthropic's Claude for data writing, OpenAI's GPT for logical analysis, and Google Gemini for long-context research within the exact same project loop
  • The Tree of Life Loop: A continuous system loop where Memory (database) feeds Intelligence (Agents), which triggers Execution (Automations), which instantly logs results back into Memory.
  • Built-in Unified Communication: Unlike standard task tools, real-time collaboration includes multiplayer cursor tracking, chat, and native video conferencing inside the workspace, removing the need for external Zoom links.

What is Taskade?

Taskade is an AI-powered collaborative workspace designed for teams to build mind maps, documentation, and project management in one unified platform. It combines project management, note-taking, and AI assistance to streamline workflows.

Target audience: Remote teams, distributed workforces, knowledge workers, creative teams, startup environments.

Taskade Value Proposition

🎯 Core Value

"One unified workspace for team collaboration, autonomous AI agents, and project execution—eliminate tool sprawl and automate manual work."

Key Differentiators:

  • Agentic Orchestration vs. Static Copilots: Traditional PM software treats AI as an inline writing text box. Taskade treats AI as an autonomous colleague that can execute asynchronous background multi-step work loops unprompted.
  • Unmatched Cost Efficiency: For a 10-person team, legacy project suites charging per-user fees plus AI add-ons cost hundreds of dollars monthly. Taskade Pro gives the entire 10-person team frontier AI access for flat-rate pricing.
  • True Zero-Code Deployment: Generates fully functioning, self-updating internal tools and customer-facing portals that do not require software hosting infrastructure or code maintenance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can we import data from Asana/Monday?

A: Yes. Taskade features an AI-powered Import Tool. You can directly upload data from Asana, Monday.com, Notion, and Trello. Instead of weeks of manual data clean-up, Taskade's AI instantly parses your legacy files and formats them into native lists, kanban boards, or mind maps in minutes. We provide hands-on migration support for Pro and Business plans.

Q: What about offline access?

A: Taskade fully supports offline workflows. Our native apps for iOS, Android, Mac, and Windows cache your active workspaces so you can view, create, and edit tasks completely offline. Once you reconnect to the internet, all local changes automatically and seamlessly sync back to your team's live cloud workspace in real time.

Q: Does AI cost extra?

A: No, AI is natively built into the core pricing structure. Taskade does not charge a mandatory "per-user AI add-on fee" like legacy platforms. Instead, your flat-rate plan includes a massive monthly pool of shared AI credits (e.g., 50,000 credits/month on the Pro plan) to power your custom agents, prompt-to-app generations, and background automation loops. If your team exhausts their monthly pool, you can buy cheap pay-as-you-go credit top-offs without forcing an entire tier upgrade.

Q: Can we white-label Taskade?

A: Yes. You can completely white-label client portals, dashboards, and internal apps. This feature unlocks on our Business tier and higher, allowing you to add your corporate logo, custom color palettes, and point the entire workspace to your own custom web domain without requiring a locked-in Enterprise contract.

Q: How is Taskade secure?

A: Security is enterprise-grade. Taskade is fully SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. All data transmission is secured using TLS 1.2 / AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit. Paid plans include 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication), and our Enterprise tier natively integrates with SAML-based Single Sign-On (SSO) providers alongside advanced role-based permissions.

Q: Can we integrate with [tool]?

A: Yes. Taskade features a built-in workflow engine with 100+ native integrations covering major platforms like Slack, Gmail, Google Calendar, GitHub, and HubSpot. For highly specialized developer setups, our Enterprise plan supports custom webhooks, a robust REST API, and native Model Context Protocol (MCP) links to let your AI agents securely talk directly to your internal software infrastructure.

Use Cases + Ideas

Perfect Fit Scenarios

📱 Startup + Lean Teams (5-50 people)

Startups are highly sensitive to seat-based cost compounding. Taskade Pro includes up to 10 users for $16/month flat (annual billing), meaning startups can scale their headcount without their software bill scaling linearly. It completely eliminates the need for separate subscriptions to Miro (mind mapping), ChatGPT/Claude (AI writing), and Asana (tasks).`

🎨 Creative Teams + Design Studios (Agencies, Design Studios)

Creative workflows struggle with rigid, text-heavy databases. Taskade’s structural ability to instantly flip a project from a standard task checklist into a visual Mind Map or an Organizational Chart makes it perfect for brainstorming campaigns. Teams use native AI agents to dynamically generate copywriting drafts, marketing hooks, and brand concepts directly on the shared canvas.

📊 Product + Tech Teams

Great for teams mapping out feature rollouts without the bureaucratic bulk of Jira. Product managers use Mind Maps for visual roadmapping, flip to Kanban or Gantt views for sprint execution, and embed custom AI agents trained on their product documentation to auto-draft Product Requirement Documents (PRDs) and release notes.

🎓 Education, Research + Training

Taskade’s infinite-nesting outliner format allows educators to map massive curriculums visually. Because Taskade allows unlimited workspace collaborators and offers a robust Free tier alongside flat-rate class tiers, it bypasses the prohibitive per-seat licensing traps that lock schools out of Asana.

🤝 Consulting & Professional Services

Taskade’s Business Tier ($40/month flat) unlocks white-label portal sharing and custom domains. Consultants can build interactive client dashboards, use AI agents to automate routine status reporting, and securely share polished, branded workspaces with external stakeholders without paying for extra client seats.

Wrong Fit Scenarios

  • Massive Traditional Enterprises (5,000+ users): Consider Asana or Jira for scale

  • Government & Highly Regulated Sectors (HIPAA/FedRAMP): Limited compliance options; traditional tools more mature
  • Organizations with Deep Legacy Enterprise Integration Needs: Monday/Asana better; Taskade is simpler
