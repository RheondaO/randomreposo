What is Taskade?

Taskade is an AI-powered collaborative workspace designed for teams to build mind maps, documentation, and project management in one unified platform. It combines project management, note-taking, and AI assistance to streamline workflows.

Target audience: Remote teams, distributed workforces, knowledge workers, creative teams, startup environments.

Taskade Value Proposition

🎯 Core Value

"One workspace for team collaboration, AI-powered thinking, and project management - eliminate tool sprawl and boost team productivity"

Key Differentiators:

  • AI-Integrated: Built-in AI assistant for brainstorming, writing, and task automation
  • Mind Mapping: Visual organization for complex projects and ideas
  • Unified Workspace: Projects, tasks, notes, and AI in one place
  • Easy Onboarding: Intuitive interface, minimal learning curve
  • Real-time Collaboration: Live editing, commenting, and team coordination
  • Flexible Pricing: Affordable entry point for small to mid-size teams

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can we import data from Asana/Monday?

A: Yes. We support CSV imports and have migration templates for common tools. Most migrations complete in 2-3 weeks. We provide migration assistance for Pro + customers.

Q: What about offline access?

A: Taskade syncs in real-time but requires internet. Native apps cache recent content for offline browsing (read-only). No native offline editing like desktop apps, but web-first teams don't need it.

Q: Does AI cost extra?

A: No. AI is included in all paid plans. Free users get limited AI (5 messages/month). Pro+ get unlimited AI usage.

Q: Can we white-label Taskade?

A: Yes, but only on Enterprise plans. Contact sales for custom branding options.

Q: How is Taskade secure?

A: SOC 2 Type II, GDPR compliant, AES-256 encryption, 2FA support, SSO (Enterprise). Data centers in US, EU, APAC regions.

Q: Can we integrate with [tool]?

A: 500+ integrations via Zapier. Native integrations with Slack, GitHub, Salesforce. Custom integrations available on Enterprise.

Use Cases & Scenarios

Perfect Fit Scenarios

📱 Startup (5-50 people)

Your team needs project management, docs, brainstorming, and AI help - all cheap, all fast, zero training. Taskade replaces 5 separate subscriptions.

🎨 Creative Teams (Agencies, Design Studios)

Mind maps replace Miro for concepts. Brainstorming is native. Tasks are organized. One app for creative workflows + AI copywriting/concepting.

📊 Product Teams

Roadmapping via mind maps. Development via kanban. Docs integrated. AI helps with requirement writing and PRD drafting. Everything in one place.

🎓 Education & Training

Teachers organize curriculum via mind maps. Students collaborate on projects. AI assists with lesson planning. One workspace for educational coordination.

🤝 Consulting & Professional Services

Project proposals as mind maps. Client work coordinated in kanban. Documentation native. AI assists with report writing and scoping.

Wrong Fit Scenarios

  • Enterprise (5,000+ users): Consider Asana or Jira for scale
  • Heavy DevOps (CI/CD heavy): Jira is better; Taskade is workflow-light on technical DevOps
  • Government/Highly Regulated: Limited compliance options; traditional tools more mature
  • Organizations with 20+ custom integrations: Monday/Asana better; Taskade is simpler
