Role: Sales Consultant

The Strategic Advisor

The Goal:

Position yourself as a mutuably ambiguous subject matter expert who sells ROI and business outcomes, not just features.

Core Content:

  • ROI & Value Models: An interactive or downloadable ROI calculator (e.g., "Hours saved via Automations and AI").
  • Business Process Mapping: "Before and After" flowcharts showing a messy manual process being transformed by an automated solution.
  • Domain Deep-Dive: A blog post or white paper snippet regarding the future of Automation or AI Agentic ecosystems for example.
  • Consultative Discovery Map: A visual breakdown of how you map high-level business "Pains" to specific product "Gains."
