Strategic Discovery (What we call 'Requirement Elicitation' & 'Scoping' where I come from)

These questions were synthesized using AI to ensure they align with Challenger and Command of the Message sales methodologies. I use AI to simulate stakeholder pushback, allowing me to refine the discovery, and present an acute and outcome-oriented approach.

  1. If we maintain the current manual process and fail a regulatory audit or miss a critical risk flag, what is the projected fiscal impact on our brand equity and stock price over the following 18 months?
  2. Beyond the immediate $273M savings identified in projects like RACR, how does automating this compliance cycle impact your department's ability to pivot toward more innovative, revenue-generating work?
  3. What internal 'Hurdle Rate' or IRR (Internal Rate of Return) does the Finance team require for a software project to be moved from a 'Nice-to-Have' to a 'Must-Fund' priority?
  4. If we achieve an 11-month payback period as projected in our ROI model, where would those reclaimed budgetary funds be most effectively reinvested within your organization?
  5. Who are the silent stakeholders in the 'No Decision' scenario? Who loses the most if this project is deferred to next year?
  6. How much of your current operational budget is being 'leaked' through manual labor hours that could be reallocated to high-yield strategic initiatives?
  7. In your experience, what is the most common reason a 'Board-Ready' business case gets rejected at the final signature stage in this organization?
