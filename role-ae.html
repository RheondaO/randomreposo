Key Resources & Support

  • 🎓 Training Materials

    Product deep-dives, sales methodology videos, and competitive battlecards available in shared repository.

  • 👥 Mentorship

    Assigned mentor (top performer) for weekly 1-on-1 calls. Manager coaching sessions every 2 weeks.

  • 📊 Sales Tools

    Salesforce access, Outreach for prospecting, Chorus for call recording, HubSpot for account tracking.

  • 🔥 Deal Support

    Sales engineer support on calls, legal review support for contracts, marketing collateral library access.

  • 📱 Peer Learning

    Weekly AE cohort calls, access to top performer call recordings, peer shadowing opportunities.

  • 📈 Performance Tracking

    Weekly activity dashboards, monthly pipeline reviews, real-time feedback from manager and peers.

  • 🎯 Territory Assets

    Account intelligence data, prospect lists, firmographic data, and competitive intel for assigned territory.

  • 💬 Communication

    Slack channels for questions, #ramp-up-ae channel for cohort support, open manager door policy.
