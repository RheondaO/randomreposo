## The Prompt
Act as a Senior SalesOps Engineer and Strategic Analyst.

Task: Analyze the provided Company Website URL/10-K Text to identify key indicators of Winnability based on our custom Ideal Customer Profile (ICP).

Variables to Evaluate (on Scale of 1-10): 
- 1. Technographic Fit: Does the company currently utilize XYZ software or similar SaaS tools?
- 2. Firmographic Momentum: Is there a documented headcount growth of more than 20% in critical departments like Legal, Compliance, or IT?
- 3. Capitalization: Has the organization recently secured Series C or D funding?
- 4. Regulatory Pressure: Does the company operate in high-stakes verticals like FinTech or Healthcare?
- 5.  Identify recent initiatives or executive quotes regarding digital transformation or security audits.

Calculation Logic: Apply the following weighted formula to the scores:

(Technographic Fit x 0.40) + (Firmographic Momentum x 0.40) + (Intent x 0.20)

Output Requirement: Provide a unified Winnability Score (1–10), a brief justification for each variable, and a recommendation for lead routing (e.g., "Route to Senior AE - High Priority")

## Logic Breakdown
The Winnability algorithm is designed to move beyond static closed-won statistics by qualifying leads aggressively early in the cycle.

- Technographic Fit (40% Weight): Validates modern stack maturity. High scores indicate the prospect has the "connective tissue" required for enterprise-grade integrations like the `sync_engine.py` middleware.
- Firmographic Momentum (40% Weight): Identifies "budgetary fuel." Growth in headcount signals an immediate need for "Painkiller" products to manage expanding risk.
- Intent (20% Weight): Acts as a trigger for real-time engagement. This ensures the sales team capitalizes on speed-to-lead opportunities.

### What is Winnability?:
The calculated probability of closing a deal, used to prioritize opportunities and maximize sales velocity. It moves beyond "closed won" stats, focusing on identifying the best opportunities early, qualifying aggressively to avoid time-wasting, and delivering maximum value, with top performers consistently focusing on value articulation.
