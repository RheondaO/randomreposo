Role: Solutions Engineer

The Technical Architect

The Goal:

Demonstrate that you can handle the "heavy lifting" of technical validation and integration.

Core Content:

  • Architecture Diagrams: Visual representations of how you’ve integrated AI or Automation tools into existing legacy infrastructures.
  • Proof of Value (POV) Tracker: A redacted template or case study showing how you define technical success criteria with a prospect.
  • The "Tech Stack" Ribbon: A focus on APIs, Webhooks, Python/Javascript snippets, and Security/Data Privacy (SOC2/GDPR) expertise.
  • Documentation Samples: Examples of "Implementation Guides" or technical FAQs you’ve authored to bridge the gap between sales and engineering.
